CMPD Community Outreach

This map shows where representatives of CMPD had outreach events in the community from 2005-2018. These events could be elementary school visits, charity fundraisers, or neighborhood crime watch talks.

The blue rectangle shows, at its center, the event that had the highest concentration of events nearby.

This map combines the outreach map with the CMPD officer involved shootings map, 2005-2018.
