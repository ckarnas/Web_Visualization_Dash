Project Title: CMPD Officer Involved Shooting Maps 2005-18

The purpose of this project was to gather the data from public databases regarding the location of CMPD officer involved police shootings and represent the locations on a map. The orange rectangle has at its center the shooting with the highest concentration of shootings nearby. The human marker signifies the location of the shooting of Keith Lamont Scott--whose shooting sparked city-wide protest, national media attention, and was the catalyst for this emploratory project.

In order to accomplish this task, we used Python to access the public database through APIs, launch a google map tool, and plotted the information.

The website allows the user to explore officer involved shooting data by year.
