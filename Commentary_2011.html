Summary: CMPD Officer Involved Shooting Maps 2005-11

This map shows CMPD officer involved shootings from the years 2005-2008. The orange rectangle marks, at its center, the shooting with the highest concentration of surrounding shootings within .02 degrees longitude and .02 degrees latitude.

The user is encouraged to click on the map in order to pull up a full-page interactive map. The full-page map allows the user to hover their mouse over specific shooting sites to reveal if the shooting resulted in a miss, a non-fatal injury, or a fatality.
